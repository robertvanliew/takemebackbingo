How to play

Bingo, but the numbers are the songs you grew up on.

Same rules as the bingo your grandma plays. The host calls songs instead of numbers, the DJ plays them, and every card is unique. First to line up five wins the round. We run three rounds a night, then close with a dance battle.

  1. 01

    Grab your card

    Each player gets a unique 5×5 card. Every square is a song title pulled from that night’s theme, 90s R&B, 2000s Hip‑Hop, Michael Jackson, 80s Throwbacks. Sixty songs in the pool. Twenty‑four on your card. One free space in the middle.

  2. 02

    The DJ drops a jam!

    Thirty to sixty seconds of a track. No host calling B‑12. If the song is on your card, you mark the square. If it’s not, you sing along until the next one drops. Could be Thriller. Could be No Scrubs. Could be a deep cut.

  3. 03

    Line it up, shout it out

    Five in a row, column, diagonal, or four corners. First person to a line yells “BINGO” (or “THRILLER,” or whatever the night’s call phrase is). Host confirms, the room cheers, the round closes.

  4. 04

    Win prizes, stay for the dance battle

    Prizes every round, bar tabs, merch, brand samples. New cards between rounds. After round six, the cards go away and the last hour is a full dance off with hosts picking a champion.

Themes we run

90s R&B · 2000s Hip‑Hop · Michael Jackson · 80s Throwbacks · Quiet Storm · Y2K Pop. New themes drop monthly.

Pace of the night

Friday, Saturday, or Sunday nights, start times vary by venue, so check the events page. Three rounds spread across about three hours, then a dance battle to close.

What to bring

Yourself, your ID (twenty‑one and over), and the lyrics you forgot you knew. Cards, daubers, and the soundtrack are on us.

FAQ · Liner Notes

The Music Bingo FAQ. Liner notes for the curious.

Everything you wanted to ask before buying a ticket to a Take Me Back Bingo night. Still curious? Send us a note.

  1. How does music bingo work?

    Music bingo works like classic bingo, but instead of numbers the DJ plays a 30-second clip of a song and the title appears on your card. Mark off songs as you hear them. First player to line up five in a row, column, or diagonal yells the call phrase and wins the round. We run three rounds a night.

  2. Do I need to know every song to win?

    No, the song title appears on the screen behind the host, so you just match it to the squares on your card. You’re playing bingo, not Shazam. Knowing the words is a bonus for singing along, not a requirement for winning.

  3. How long is a music bingo night?

    A Take Me Back Bingo night runs about three hours. We host on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays, start times vary by venue, so check the events page for the night you want. Each night is three bingo rounds plus a live dance battle to close.

  4. How much does a music bingo ticket cost?

    Ticket prices vary by city and venue, most general-admission seats run $15–$25. Reserved group tables with bottle service start higher. Check the Events page for live pricing and date-specific tiers.

  5. Is music bingo 21+? Do I need an ID?

    Yes, every Take Me Back Bingo night is 21 and over with no exceptions. Bring a valid government-issued photo ID, driver’s license, passport, or state ID. The door checks IDs as soon as it opens and won’t admit anyone without it.

  6. Can I bring a group to music bingo?

    Yes, groups of six or more can reserve a high-top with bottle service and priority cards. We’ve hosted birthdays, bachelorettes, and work crews. Use the booking form to reserve a table; otherwise, general admission seating is first-come, first-served.

  7. Can you bring music bingo to my city or company?

    We travel for private events, brand activations, corporate parties, festivals, and weddings. If there’s a sound system and a crowd, we can build a custom show with custom themes, custom cards, and custom prizes. Reach out via the booking form to start the conversation.

  8. What if I show up late to music bingo?

    Start times vary by venue, check the events page for your night. Aim to arrive about 15 minutes before the first round so you can grab a drink, pick up your cards, and find a seat. If you arrive after a round has started, you’ll jump in mid-game, we don’t hold cards or pause for late arrivals.

  9. Can I get a refund on a music bingo ticket?

    All ticket sales are final, no refunds and no transfers. If Take Me Back Bingo cancels a night for any reason, you’ll receive a full refund automatically. Outside of that, plan ahead and double-check the date before you buy. Questions? info@takemebackbingo.com.

  10. What do you wear to music bingo?

    Dress like it’s 2003, throwback jerseys, denim on denim, velour tracksuits, gold chains, chunky sneakers, baby tees. The photos hit harder when the room looks like a time machine. There’s no formal dress code, so whatever you’d wear to a good bar also works.
