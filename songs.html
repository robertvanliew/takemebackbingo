The Take Me Back Bingo Nights tracklist.

Music bingo at Take Me Back Bingo Nights is hip-hop and R&B, sorted by decade. The DJ pulls 30-second clips from songs like the ones below. If the song is on your card when the DJ drops the needle, you daub the square. Five in a row, yell “TAKE ME BACK,” you win the round.

Below: 150 tracks across six themed pools, from Sade and Run‑DMC through Boyz II Men and Biggie, all the way to Beyoncé and 50 Cent. Try the full game free at takemebackbingo.com/play.
